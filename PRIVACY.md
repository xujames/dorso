# Privacy Policy for Dorso

**Last Updated: January 25, 2025**

## Overview

Dorso is a macOS menu bar application that monitors your posture using your computer's camera. Your privacy is important to us, and Dorso is designed with privacy as a core principle.

## Data Collection

**Dorso does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### Camera Usage

- Dorso accesses your camera solely to detect your posture in real-time
- Video frames are processed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework
- No images or video are ever stored, recorded, or transmitted
- Camera data never leaves your computer

### No Analytics

- Dorso contains no analytics or tracking software
- No usage data is collected
- No crash reports are sent

### No Network Access

- Dorso does not connect to the internet
- No data is transmitted to any servers
- The app functions completely offline

### No Account Required

- Dorso does not require account creation
- No personal information is requested or stored

## Third-Party Services

Dorso does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs.

## Data Storage

Dorso does not store any user data. Your calibration settings exist only in memory while the app is running and are not persisted to disk.

## Children's Privacy

Dorso does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above.
